About Linnaeus

Biology is an increasingly important field that is demanding more and more computation.



Linnaeus provides a modern, scalable solution to the challenges facing computational biology. Aimed toward persons wishing to figure out their family's past, the risks that diseases pose to the present, or high school and college students,

Linnaeus enhances learning and gives you the chance to explore advanced visual concepts with serious tools.

What is a pedigree?


A pedigree is a visual representation of a line of ancestors. It can be used to record the history of genetic diseases in a family lineage, as well as to calculate the probability of a certain individual being afflicted with a disease.

Females in a pedigree are represented with circles, while males are represented with squares. A horizontal line indicates the union of two individuals, with vertical lines leading to their offspring. A shaded circle/square indicates that an individual is afflicted with a disease.

What is a Punnett Square?


A Punnett Square is a visual representation of the potential offspring of two parents.
